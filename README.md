Welcome to TechManas
Human-centered cyber wellness and resilience.

Welcome to TechManas, a dedicated cyberpsychology platform designed to help you navigate the digital world safely. We exist at the intersection of online behavior, digital risk, and emotional well-being.

Whether you are looking to build healthier screen habits, need immediate guidance after a stressful online incident, or want to safeguard your organization, TechManas provides practical, human-centered support.

🧭 How TechManas Can Help You
Our platform is built on three core pillars to support you at every stage of your digital life:

🛡️ 1. Preventive: Build Safer Habits
Identify risks early and understand your digital behavior before issues arise.

Cyber Wellness Assessment: Take a quick, confidential screening to evaluate your digital stress, privacy awareness, and screen-time habits.

Awareness Micro-Learning: Browse quick guides on cyber hygiene, digital empathy, and online safety.

Parent & Educator Toolkit: Access behavioral indicators and age-appropriate conversation starters to guide children and students safely online.

🆘 2. Reactive: Immediate Support After an Incident
If you or someone you know has experienced a digitally mediated harm, we provide step-by-step guidance to help you respond and recover.

Incident Support Center: Get customized advice for dealing with cyberbullying, online harassment, stalking, phishing-induced anxiety, or online fraud trauma.

Guided Checklists: Clear, actionable steps on what to do in the next 10 minutes.

Evidence Preservation: Platform-specific guidance on what to screenshot, what not to delete, and how to safely document an incident.

Escalation Pathways: Connect with the right professional support, from reporting tools to certified helplines.

🌱 3. Proactive: Grow Your Cyber Resilience
Develop long-term readiness and set healthy digital boundaries.

Resilience Planner: Build a personalized plan to manage your digital footprint, establish screen boundaries, and recover from digital overwhelm.

Organization Dashboard: For HR and school administrators to evaluate institutional readiness, track policy status, and prioritize cyber-awareness training.

Resource Library: Access a curated, filterable collection of guides, templates, and expert articles.

👤 Who is TechManas For?
TechManas is designed to provide specific tools for different needs:

Individuals: Manage digital stress, recover from online conflicts, and build a personalized wellness plan.

Parents & Educators: Understand what your children are facing online and learn how to initiate healthy conversations about digital boundaries.

Organizations & Schools: Assess your institution's cyber-culture, provide employee/student support, and build robust digital policies.
